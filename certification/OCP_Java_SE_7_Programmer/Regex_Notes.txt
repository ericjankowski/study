Regex Notes

\d - A digit (0-9)
\D - A non-digit (anything but 0-9)

\s - A whitespace character (e.g. space, \t, \n, \f, \r)
\S - A non-whitespace character

\w - A word character (letters (a-z and A-Z), digits, or the underscore)
\W - A non-word character

\b - A word boundary (ends of the string and between \w and \W characters)
\B - A non-word boundary (between two \w's or two \W's) 

* - Zero or more occurrences
? - Zero or one occurrence
+ - One or more occurrences

[] - Specifies a range - [abc] - [a-f] - [a-zA-Z]
